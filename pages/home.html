Hard Surfaces

We offer granite from around the world, as well as from quarries in Georgia, New York, and the Mid-West. Beautiful and practical, granite can be used for far more than counter tops. Seamless granite is stunning in showers surrounds, or installed around a fireplace. We can help you select just the right piece to make an island or a bar top a work of art.

Edge profiles can add the perfect finish. Along with the standard edge profiles, we can shape the edges with a beveled or ogee profile. Other hard surface options include quartz, marble, and Cesar Stone.

Every kitchen, bathroom, and bar needs a sink. We have porcelain undermount sinks, stainless steel, copper, and bronze—and farmhouse sinks in porcelain and stainless steel.

Cabinets

Our custom-designed cabinets can be ordered, installed, and finished within 10 days. Not only are they a staple of a beautiful and function kitchen, our cabinets will transform a simple bathroom vanity.

Other applications include:

  • Home Offices
  • Built-in storage for living rooms, dining rooms, and play rooms
  • How about a window seat from which to watch the world go by?
  • Entertainment Centers
